[Submitted on 26 Jan 2025]

Part I · 概览与背景

核心贡献

  • 1In order to improve these parameter estimation procedures for estimating {\tau}, we developed a simulation-extrapolation (SIMEX) methodology in the context of time series analysis
  • 2We consider both the standard class of autoregressive processes observed at regular intervals and a recently developed class of irregularly autoregressive processes (iAR)
